Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

Q: How do I create a task?

A: Just below where it says "username" to-do list, there is a button to create a task.

Q: How do I edit a task?

A: Right next to the view and delete button, there is an edit button so you can edit your task and description.

Q: How do I delete a task?

A: Right after you've created a task, it will have two options: either to view your tasks and delete that task.

Account Questions

Q: How do I create an account?

A: In the top right corner, there is a sign-up button. It will ask for the username you want and a strong password so you can start keeping track of tasks!

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: You can't. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Technical Questions

Q: What should I do if I encounter an error?

A: Cry.

Q: Can I access this app on mobile devices?

A: Unfortunately, TaskFlow is only available on desktop. Hopefully, it will reach mobile devices soon.

If you have more questions, please contact us.
